\# 👩‍💼 **Employee  Salary  Analysis  Dashboard**



\## 📌 **Objective**

The goal  of  this  project  is  to  analyze  employee  salary  data  to  understand  salary  distribution ,  department-wise  trends ,  and  key  factors  affecting  compensation.



\## 📂 **Dataset**

\- Employee  dataset  including  salary ,  department ,  role ,  and  experience

\- Data  cleaned  and  structured  for  analysis



\## 🛠 **Tools \& Techniques Used**

\-Microsoft  Excel

\- Pivot  Tables

\- Charts

\-Slicers



\## 📈 **Key Insights**

\- Salary varies significantly across departments

\- Experience has a direct impact on salary growth

\- Identified differences in salary distribution



\## 📊 **Dashboard Features**

\-Department-wise salary comparison

\- Experience vs salary analysis
